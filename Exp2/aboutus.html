ABOUT US

Kerala State Road Transport Corporation, also known as KSRTC was founded on 1937 with an aim to reorganize the state's transport service. The first Superintendent was Mr E. G. Salter who was Assistant Operating Superintendent of the London Passenger Transport Board. It is divided in three zones, namely South Zone, Central Zone, and North Zone. KSRTC also has earned a reputation of commuting over 3.145 million passengers on a daily basis.
